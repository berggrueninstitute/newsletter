Nicolas Berggruen: Arts and Culture Offer Us New Narratives for a Better World

On the basis of what ideas will we build a better world? In this bylined piece for PBS’ KCET, Nicolas Berggruen argues that change happens through people and their ideas and that today’s resistance movement needs the same assets developed by every successful movement in the past: leadership, organization, a plan, and behind them all — a narrative.

Jared Diamond: Global Pandemic and Crisis Management

As part of the Global Thinkers Series, author Jared Diamond contextualizes the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic in his new book Upheaval: Turning Points for Nations in Crisis.
